# 06 - Trust & Compliance Signals: Competitor Analysis

> How the eight competitor sites in the peptide / research chemical vertical build trust with buyers. Data gathered April 2026.

| Vendor | COA Presentation | Per-Batch or Per-Product? | Location on Site | Format | Quality Assessment |
|--------|-----------------|--------------------------|-------------------|--------|-------------------|
| **Amino Asylum** | COA listed on product pages; purity data included per listing | Per-product | Product detail pages | PDF / inline data | Provides COAs with HPLC-MS data. COA quality has been questioned by some community members. No named lab partner publicly disclosed. |
| **Peptide Sciences** (shut down) | COA provided per product; testing appeared internal | Per-product | Product pages, dedicated purity info page | PDF download | Testing was internal, NOT independent third-party. Customers had no way to verify COA authenticity independently. A key weakness noted by reviewers. Purity shown as 99.3% on COAs, lower than some competitors at 99.8%. [Source](https://www.peptidesciences.com/peptide-information/peptide-purity/) |
| **Core Peptides** | COAs present but not universally included | Partially per-batch | Product pages (not all) | PDF download | COAs are not consistently included with all shipments. Testing documentation only partially accessible on website. [Source](https://www.peptideprotocolwiki.com/vendors/core-peptides) |
| **Sports Technology Labs** | COAs publicly accessible on dedicated page and on each product page | Per-batch | Dedicated /coas/ page + individual product pages | PDF download with lab contact info | **Best-in-class transparency.** All COAs produced by named lab (MZ Biolabs). Lab contact info included so customers can verify independently. [Source](https://sportstechnologylabs.com/coas/) |
| **Limitless Biotech** | COAs available on website; third-party tested | Per-batch (claimed) | Product pages, quality statement page | PDF download | Each batch tested with HPLC purity, LC-MS molecular confirmation, and contaminant screening. However, the testing laboratory is NOT publicly disclosed. Some popular items have been reported lacking COAs. Inconsistency concerns raised on Reddit. [Source](https://www.limitlesslifenootropics.com/quality-statement-2/) |
| **Swole AF Labs / SAF Research** | Lab results displayed on website | Unclear | Website (specific location not confirmed) | Likely PDF/image | Uses S&N Labs for testing (a popular and reliable option for SARMs vendors). Limited public documentation. |
| **Pumping Iron Store** | Only a few products have COAs | Per-product (inconsistent) | Product pages (select products only) | PDF/image | **Weakest COA transparency.** Only a few SARMs have COAs, and most that exist are outdated. Unclear why only specific products have lab tests. [Source](https://sarmsreviews.to/pumpingironstore-com/) |
| **Loti Labs** | COA provided with each product shipment | Per-batch | Product pages + shipped with orders | PDF download | Every batch tested by US accredited laboratory using HPLC. COA included with shipment. Minimum 98% purity guaranteed. [Source](https://lotilabs.com/) |

### Key Finding
Sports Technology Labs sets the standard with named lab (MZ Biolabs), batch-specific COAs, and a dedicated COA page. Most vendors provide COAs but vary widely in completeness, recency, and verifiability.

### Industry Standard: Janoshik Analytical
[Janoshik](https://janoshik.com/) is widely considered the "gold standard" for gray-market peptide/SARM testing. Key features:
- Blind testing accuracy
- QR-verified COAs that catch forgeries
- 10+ years of consistent results
- Used by Chinese manufacturers for guarantee claims
- **Limitations:** No ISO 17025 accreditation; COAs lack methodology details; raw data behind paywall
- Trustpilot: Mixed reviews but generally respected

None of the eight competitors explicitly use Janoshik on their product COAs, though individual buyers may submit products for independent Janoshik testing.

## 4. Purity Claims

| Vendor | Exact Language | Notes |
|--------|---------------|-------|
| **Amino Asylum** | "High purity levels" | No specific percentage prominently displayed in search results. |
| **Peptide Sciences** | "99%+ purity" / "Buy [Product] \| 99% Purity (USA Made)" | Used "99% Purity" in every product title. Actual COA showed 99.3%. [Source](https://www.peptidesciences.com/bpc-157) |
| **Core Peptides** | "99% purity" / ">99% purity" / "purity levels consistently exceeding 99%" | Strong purity claims. Used in product descriptions and about page. [Source](https://www.corepeptides.com/peptides/bpc-157/) |
| **Sports Technology Labs** | SARMs: "minimum 98% purity." Peptides: "minimum 95% purity" (when Mannitol required) or "minimum 98% purity" (without Mannitol) | Most nuanced and honest purity claims. Explains why some peptides are 95% (mannitol preservative). [Source](https://sportstechnologylabs.com/peptides/) |
| **Limitless Biotech** | "highest purity standards" / ">=98.5% purity" (non-lyophilized) / "ultra pure, pharmaceutical grade" | Uses "pharmaceutical grade" language. Specifies 98.5% minimum for non-lyophilized products. [Source](https://limitlesslifenootropics.com/) |
| **Swole AF Labs** | "quality, third-party tested" | No specific percentage prominently stated. |
| **Pumping Iron Store** | "highest quality compounds" | Generic quality claims without specific purity percentages. |
| **Loti Labs** | "99%+ purity" / "independently lab-tested to guarantee 99%+ purity" / "guaranteeing minimum 98% purity" (per-product) | Strong claims backed by per-batch testing. [Source](https://lotilabs.com/) |

### Language Patterns
- **"99%+ purity"** is the most common claim (Peptide Sciences, Core Peptides, Loti Labs)
- **"pharmaceutical grade"** used by Limitless Biotech
- **"research grade"** used by Sports Technology Labs, Loti Labs
- **"USP-grade solvents"** specified by Sports Technology Labs
- Sports Technology Labs is the only vendor that explains purity variance (95% vs 98%) due to preservative requirements

## Recommendations for AzAminos Trust Strategy

Based on competitive analysis, the strongest trust stack would combine:

1. **Named third-party lab** on every COA (follow Sports Technology Labs model with MZ Biolabs)
2. **Dedicated /coa/ or /lab-results/ page** with searchable, batch-specific PDFs
3. **Trustpilot integration** as the primary review platform (industry standard)
4. **Specific purity claims** with honest variance explanations (not blanket "99%+")
5. **BBB accreditation** (Limitless Biotech is the only one; easy differentiation)
6. **cGMP manufacturing claim** with named facility or facility certifications
7. **60-day money-back guarantee** (matches or exceeds Sports Technology Labs)
8. **Low free-shipping threshold** ($99 like Loti Labs, not $350 like Limitless)
9. **"Made in USA"** prominently in site title/header (Core Peptides and Peptide Sciences model)
10. **Same-day shipping** with tracking and clear cutoff time (Loti Labs: 1 PM EST)
