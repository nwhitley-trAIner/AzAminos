## I) Naming Convention Patterns

How competitors refer to GLP-1 products (the hottest category):

```
- "GLP-1" (generic category)
- "Reta" / "GLP-3" / "Retatrutide" (Loti Labs uses abbreviated + class name)
- "Tirz" / "GLP-2" / "Tirzepatide" (Loti Labs)
- "Semaglutide" (most sites use full research name)
- "GLP-1 Receptor Agonist" (Limitless Biotech, scientific framing)
```

How competitors name categories:
```
- By research area: "Cognitive Health," "Metabolic," "Tissue Regeneration" (Limitless Biotech)
- By compound type: "Peptides," "SARMs," "Research Liquids" (most sites)
- By use case: "Recovery Compounds," "Growth Compounds" (Direct Peptides)
- Abbreviated: "Reta," "Tirz" (Loti Labs — most informal)
```
