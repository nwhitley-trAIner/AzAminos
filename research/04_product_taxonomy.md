### Bundling / Kit Patterns Observed

1. **Pre-blended vials** (most common): Two or three peptides lyophilized together in one vial. Eliminates mixing for the buyer. Core Peptides and Loti Labs lean heavily on this.
2. **Multi-vial stacks**: Separate vials sold together at a discount. Sports Tech Labs does this.
3. **All-in-one capsules**: Pumping Iron Store's "Arnie" puts five SARMs in one capsule. Swole AF sells capsules-only.
4. **Volume discounts**: Sports Tech Labs: 40% off for 30+ items, 50% off for 50+ items (wholesale). Loti Labs: buy 2 get 1 free on same product. Peptide Sciences (historical): tiered per-unit pricing on 3/5/10 qty.
5. **Subscription/auto-ship**: Amino Asylum offers 20% savings on auto-ship.
6. **Starter kits**: Some vendors bundle bacteriostatic water + syringes with first peptide purchase.

## Key Takeaways

1. **BPC-157 is the universal anchor product** -- every single active vendor stocks it, and it's the top seller in the vertical.
2. **GLP-1 agonists (semaglutide, tirzepatide) are the fastest-growing category** and command premium prices, especially retatrutide.
3. **CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin blend is the #1 pre-made combo** across peptide vendors.
4. **SARMs and peptides are increasingly sold by the same vendors** but some sites still specialize (Pumping Iron = SARMs-focused; Core Peptides = peptides-focused).
5. **Capsule and nasal spray formats are differentiators** -- Swole AF is capsule-only; Limitless Biotech offers sprays, capsules, ampoules, and injectables.
6. **Pre-blended vials are a major trend** -- vendors combine 2-3 peptides in a single vial (Wolverine Stack, Glow Stack, GH blends) for convenience and higher AOV.
7. **Volume/wholesale discounts are standard** -- most vendors offer tiered pricing at 3/5/10 unit breaks or a buy-2-get-1 model.
8. **Supply cross-selling is an underserved opportunity** -- most peptide vendors do minimal supply bundling, leaving revenue to Amazon and dedicated supply stores.
