## Key Observations

1. **Payment fragmentation is extreme** — No two vendors use the same payment stack. Crypto acceptance is nearly universal among top vendors (BTC, ETH, USDC). Credit cards are available but through high-risk processors, not Stripe/PayPal/Square.
2. **Vendor churn is high** — At least 4 of the original 10 competitors listed are now defunct or redirecting (Amino Asylum, Swole AF Labs, Pumping Iron Store, Peptide Sciences).
3. **GLP-1s are the hot category** — Semaglutide and Tirzepatide are featured prominently, but Tirzepatide is disappearing from many vendors due to Eli Lilly legal pressure.
4. **Trust differentiation** is via COAs and third-party testing — the vendors with the highest ratings all emphasize batch-specific, third-party HPLC/MS testing.
5. **"Research use only" framing is universal** — Every active vendor positions products exclusively for laboratory/research purposes.
